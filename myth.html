Cancer Myth Buster

Breaking the myths and unveiling the facts about cancer

Myth 1: Cancer is always a death sentence

This myth often causes unnecessary fear, but cancer treatment has greatly improved over the years.

Fact: Many cancers are treatable!

With early detection and advanced treatments, many types of cancer are now curable or manageable for years.

Myth 2: Cancer is contagious

Some believe that cancer can spread from person to person, which creates stigma and isolation.

Fact: Cancer cannot be caught from others

Cancer is not contagious. It is caused by genetic mutations, lifestyle factors, or environmental influences.
